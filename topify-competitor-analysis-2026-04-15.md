Top 5 Topify.ai competitors checked on 2026-04-15

Method:
- I focused on tools with the closest overlap in AI visibility / GEO / AEO tracking and optimization.
- I checked public pricing pages plus public customer-review pages, mainly G2.
- Topify baseline used for comparison: https://topify.ai/ and https://topify.ai/pricing

Quick market read

The five closest public-market competitors I found are Profound, Scrunch AI, Peec AI, Otterly.AI, and Promptwatch. They all sell some combination of AI search visibility tracking, prompt monitoring, citation analysis, competitor benchmarking, and optimization workflows. The split in the market is clear:

- Enterprise-heavy tools: Profound, Scrunch
- Mid-market/self-serve monitoring tools: Peec, Otterly, Promptwatch

Topify's opening is that it is priced like a self-serve tool but is already positioning beyond monitoring into execution: prompt discovery, citation reverse-engineering, competitor benchmarking, article generation, and one-click answer execution.

1. Profound

Pricing and features:
- G2 pricing page shows Starter at $99/month, Growth at $399/month, and Enterprise/custom tiers above that: https://www.g2.com/products/profound/pricing
- Public positioning is strong enterprise AI visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Copilot, Grok, and agent monitoring.

What customers complain about:
- G2 review themes point to inconsistent data freshness/consistency and a dashboard that can feel less mature than the promise: https://www.g2.com/products/profound/reviews
- Complaint pattern: some users like the concept but still want better reliability and a more polished analytics experience.

Biggest weakness vs Topify:
- Profound looks like an enterprise intelligence platform first and an action tool second. It is harder to justify for smaller teams if they need fast execution, not a long sales cycle and dashboard review process.

What Topify should say on a sales call:
- "If you want AI visibility without buying a heavy enterprise platform, Topify gets you in at $99 and gives you execution levers, not just monitoring."

2. Scrunch AI

Pricing and features:
- Official pricing page shows Core at $250/month with 125 prompts, 5 site audits, 5 user licenses, and tracking for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Copilot; Enterprise is custom: https://scrunch.com/pricing/
- It is clearly positioned around enterprise AI search monitoring, site audits, prompt tracking, and team workflows.

What customers complain about:
- G2 reviewers praise the category fit but mention UI complexity and a learning curve before teams get full value: https://www.g2.com/products/scrunch-ai/reviews
- Complaint pattern: feature-rich, but not always lightweight or instantly usable for lean teams.

Biggest weakness vs Topify:
- Scrunch starts at a much higher entry price than Topify and feels built for larger teams doing structured monitoring programs, not fast-moving marketing teams that want affordable GEO wins now.

What Topify should say on a sales call:
- "Scrunch is priced like an enterprise monitoring suite. Topify is easier to buy, faster to onboard, and better for teams that want to move from insight to action this week."

3. Peec AI

Pricing and features:
- G2 pricing lists Starter at $95/month, Pro at $245/month, Advanced at $495/month, and Enterprise/custom above that: https://www.g2.com/products/peec-ai/pricing
- Peec focuses on AI search visibility tracking, brand/citation monitoring, competitor comparisons, and reporting. Review hub: https://www.g2.com/products/peec-ai/reviews

What customers complain about:
- G2 review themes mention missing or limited integrations, a desire for stronger workflow automation, and some manual effort in reporting/export use cases.
- Complaint pattern: solid visibility product, but customers still want a fuller operating layer around the data.

Biggest weakness vs Topify:
- Peec is strong on monitoring, but the gap is post-insight action. Topify can position harder on prompt discovery, content generation, and execution support after the visibility issue is identified.

What Topify should say on a sales call:
- "Peec helps you see the visibility problem. Topify helps you see it and act on it from the same workflow."

4. Otterly.AI

Pricing and features:
- Official pricing page shows Lite at $29/month for 15 prompts, Standard at $189/month for 100 prompts, Premium at $489/month for 400 prompts, with add-ons for AI Mode and Gemini tracking: https://otterly.ai/pricing
- Otterly covers brand visibility, domain rankings, citations, GEO audits, and reporting across ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Copilot.

What customers complain about:
- G2 reviews praise the focus but mention that costs rise quickly as teams need more prompts and broader coverage; some users also mention setup/interpretation friction: https://www.g2.com/products/otterly-ai/reviews
- Complaint pattern: good specialist tracker, but scaling usage can get expensive for what is still primarily a monitoring product.

Biggest weakness vs Topify:
- Otterly is compelling at the low end, but once usage increases the price climbs while the product still centers on tracking. Topify can argue better value if the buyer wants both monitoring and optimization motion.

What Topify should say on a sales call:
- "Otterly is a clean tracker. Topify is the better choice if you want tracking plus a workflow to improve AI answers and citations."

5. Promptwatch

Pricing and features:
- Official pricing page shows Essential at $99/month for 50 prompts, Professional at $249/month for 150 prompts, and Business at $579/month for 350 prompts: https://promptwatch.com/pricing
- Promptwatch is broad on model coverage and includes AI visibility, competitor analysis, generated articles, content optimization, and API access. It tracks OpenAI, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews and AI Mode, Claude, Gemini, Meta Llama, DeepSeek, and Grok.

What customers complain about:
- G2 reviewers raise concerns around account/admin structure and ask for stronger security controls and agency-style management: https://www.g2.com/products/promptwatch/reviews
- Complaint pattern: ambitious feature set, but organizational controls and workflow polish are not yet where some teams want them.

Biggest weakness vs Topify:
- Promptwatch is broad, but breadth can blur focus. Topify can win by being simpler and more opinionated around the specific GEO/AEO workflow a marketing team actually needs.

What Topify should say on a sales call:
- "Promptwatch covers a lot of models, but Topify is easier to operationalize if your actual goal is improving brand visibility and answer inclusion, not managing a sprawling prompt ops stack."

Where Topify is strongest right now

Based on the current public Topify site and pricing, the clearest winning messages are:

- Self-serve entry price: Topify starts at $99, which lets it compete with lower-cost tools while undercutting enterprise-first platforms.
- Execution, not just monitoring: Topify publicly emphasizes high-value prompt discovery, citation reverse-engineering, article generation, and one-click execution. That is a sharper story than "another dashboard."
- Unlimited seats on entry tiers: this is useful against tools that gate collaboration behind higher plans.
- Good mid-market positioning: many competitors either feel too enterprise and expensive or too monitoring-only.

The biggest risk for Topify

The market is getting crowded quickly, and several competitors already look stronger on one of these dimensions:

- deeper enterprise coverage
- broader model coverage
- more mature reporting/admin controls

So the best path is not trying to sound bigger than everyone else. The best path is to own one clear message:

"Topify is the fastest way for a marketing team to find AI visibility gaps and actually fix them."

Best sales positioning summary

- Against Profound: "You do not need enterprise overhead to get AI visibility and action."
- Against Scrunch: "You do not need to start at $250/month to run a serious GEO program."
- Against Peec: "Monitoring alone is not enough; we help you execute."
- Against Otterly: "As your usage grows, Topify gives you more optimization value for the spend."
- Against Promptwatch: "We are narrower, simpler, and more operational for marketing teams."
