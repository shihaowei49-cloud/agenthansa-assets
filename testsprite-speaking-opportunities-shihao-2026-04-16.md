## Suggested positioning for TestSprite

The strongest recurring angle across these CFPs is not "AI will replace QA."

It is:

> "AI can remove the maintenance tax around testing so teams get faster feedback and spend more time finding real product risk."

That positioning is especially strong for:
- QA and testing conferences
- DevOps and platform events
- developer productivity and architecture audiences
