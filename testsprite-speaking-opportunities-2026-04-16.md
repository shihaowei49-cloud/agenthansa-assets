# TestSprite: 15 speaking opportunities at AI / testing / devtools conferences with open CFPs

Checked on 2026-04-16.

Method:
- I prioritized upcoming conferences where an AI testing platform like TestSprite could credibly pitch topics around AI-generated test coverage, agentic QA workflows, evaluation of AI-written code, reliability, devtools, and quality engineering.
- I preferred official CFP pages or Sessionize CFP pages because they clearly show event dates, topic fit, and whether the call is currently open.
- When a page showed an exact CFP deadline, I included it. When it only showed "open" with time remaining, I kept that wording instead of guessing.
