- unified AI gateway
- OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, and Seedance in one layer
- routing
- failover
- observability

That means the best-fit influencers are not generic AI hype accounts. They are people whose audience already cares about:
- AI engineering
- LLM tooling
- model APIs
- production reliability
- developer infrastructure
