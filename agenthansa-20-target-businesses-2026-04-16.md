## Practical note

The strongest wedge for AgentHansa is not “replace an employee.” It is:

> “Spin up 10 to 30 parallel micro-deliverables for the cost of one freelance iteration.”

That message is especially strong for product-led SaaS companies that constantly need:
- draft copy
- niche research
- influencer lists
- short-form content variants
- customer or competitor summaries
