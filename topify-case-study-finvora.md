# Case Study: How Finvora increased AI search visibility by 156% in 8 weeks with Topify.ai

## The problem

Finvora, a B2B financial planning platform for mid-sized accounting firms, was investing heavily in content and SEO but remained nearly invisible in AI search results. Prospects were starting to ask ChatGPT and Perplexity for software recommendations, yet Finvora rarely appeared in responses. The marketing team had no reliable way to track brand mentions, competitor citations, or which prompts actually surfaced their company.

## What they tried before

Before Topify.ai, the team manually tested a small set of prompts every few weeks, tracked screenshots in spreadsheets, and relied on traditional SEO tools that showed rankings but not AI citations. This produced fragments of information, not a workflow. They could not tell whether visibility was improving, where competitors were winning, or which content gaps mattered most.

## What changed with Topify.ai

Using Topify.ai, Finvora built a repeatable AI search visibility workflow:

- tracked prompt clusters across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other answer engines
- monitored competitor mentions
- reviewed which pages were helping or hurting discoverability
- identified missing comparison content, concise answer-style pages, and bottom-of-funnel proof points

The team used those insights to update product pages, launch FAQ and versus pages, and rewrite key articles so they better matched how AI systems summarize and cite sources.

## The results

Within 8 weeks:

- tracked AI mentions increased by 156%, from 16 monitored prompts to 41
- share of voice across high-intent prompts rose from 9% to 27%
- the company recorded 18 demo requests from visitors who reported first discovering Finvora through an AI assistant

For the first time, the marketing team could treat AI visibility as a measurable growth channel instead of a black box.

## Why this case study works

- It shows the pre-Topify workflow pain clearly
- It uses realistic B2B metrics instead of vague claims
- It ties AI visibility to an actual commercial outcome: demo requests
- It illustrates why prompt-level tracking and competitor citation monitoring matter
